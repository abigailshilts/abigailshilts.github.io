Welcome to a beginners guide to ultimate frisbee. While this is by no means a complete compilation of everything you will ever need to know this is a great starting point!

Ultimate structure and culture is based around our core value, spirit of the game. What this means is that there is high levels of respect and acceptance in ultimate for the game, opponents and teammates. Ultimate is self officiated meaning it is up to the players to make their own calls and resolve disputes. As a result players have high levels of integrity and treat each other with respect. Oftentimes at the end of games both teams will come together in a combined huddle where they thank each other for the game and recognize players who exhibited exemplary spirit.
