Backhand

A backhand throw is one where you bring your arm across your body. If you are right handed this is used to throw forward or in any slightly to very leftward direction, and the opposite if you are left handed.


Forehand

A forehand (or flick) is a throw where you extend your arm out and away from your body on the side of the arm which you are using. If you are right handed it is used to throw forward or in any slightly to very rightward direction, and the opposite if you are left handed.


Other throws

While Backhand and Forehand throws are by far the most common type in ultimate and the only two you need to know there are a handful of others that can be useful or fun to know. Apart from these two hammers and scoober are also sometimes utilized in game and can be handy in a pinch. Additionally forehand and backhand throws can be varied by adding curvature to the throw.
